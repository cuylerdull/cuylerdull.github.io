AERO NU
is Northwestern's all-new aeronautics interest group that I co-founded with Lawrence Luo.
We aim to:
  • educate our members on the many facets of the aerospace industry
  • develop in our members design and engineering skills as they relate to aviation
  • compete in the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics Design Build Fly (AIAA DBF) competition in 2020 and in the years to come
Hard Skills
  • CAD (SolidWorks)
  • KeyShot
  • CFD (SimScale)
  • 3D Printing
  • laser cutting
  • hot-wire foam cutting
Soft Skills
  • Leadership
  • Communication
  • Delegation
  • Time management
  • Negotiation
